DROP YOUR PRODUCT PHOTOS HERE
=============================

Each product in products.js points to a filename in this folder.
To add a real photo:

  1. Save your photo here, e.g.  tote-marigold.jpg
  2. Make sure the filename matches the "image" field in ../products.js
  3. Refresh the page — the placeholder is replaced automatically

Filenames the page is currently looking for:
  hero.jpg              (big hero image, ~1200x1000px)
  tote-marigold.jpg
  tote-rose.jpg
  tote-indigo.jpg
  slippers-coral.jpg
  slippers-sage.jpg
  case-magenta.jpg
  case-teal.jpg
  pouch-mustard.jpg
  pouch-coral.jpg
  charm-sage.jpg

Square photos (1:1) work best for product cards.
