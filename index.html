How it's made

Six blocks. Three colours.
One you.

Every LuvEco print starts with a wooden block carved by hand in a workshop outside Jaipur — one block for each colour, pressed by hand across the cotton, hundreds of times.

The tiny shifts where the blocks meet aren't flaws. They're the fingerprints of the maker — proof that a person, not a machine, made the thing you're carrying.
