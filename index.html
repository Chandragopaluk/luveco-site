Six blocks. Three colours. One piece.

Every LuvEco print starts with a wooden block carved by hand in a workshop outside Jaipur. One block per colour, lined up by eye on the cotton, pressed hundreds of times across a single bolt.

The tiny shifts where blocks land slightly differently between repeats aren't flaws. They're the marks of the hand — proof no machine was involved.

The printed cotton is hand-quilted, cut and finished in small workshops in Sanganer, then packed in cotton — no plastic — and shipped from our UK studio.
